Help

Xyber Business Email Extractor is not affiliated with Google.

How to use

  1. Open the extension popup and enter a map search query.
  2. Click Open Maps and browse business listings.
  3. On the map page, use Start Auto Extract to collect visible listings.
  4. Click Enable website access in the popup if you want email discovery from public business websites.
  5. Open the dashboard to review leads and export CSV or TXT files.

Permissions

  • Storage — keeps collected leads in your browser.
  • Website access (optional) — visits public business websites linked from listings to find published contact emails.
