Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This policy describes how Xyber Business Email Extractor (“the Extension”) handles information. The Extension is published by Xyber and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google.

What data the Extension processes

  • Public business listing details visible while you browse map search results (for example: business name, phone, address, website, ratings).
  • Email addresses and social links found on public business websites when you use email discovery.
  • Your search queries entered in the Extension popup.

Where data is stored

Collected leads are stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage. The Extension does not upload your lead lists to Xyber servers for storage.

Website access permission

If you enable email discovery, the Extension may request permission to visit public business websites linked from map listings. This permission is optional and only used to find publicly published contact emails on those sites.

Data sharing

We do not sell your scraped data. Data leaves your device only when you export or download files (CSV, TXT, XLSX).

Third-party services

The Extension works on third-party websites including map providers and business websites. Your use of those services remains subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Google Maps is a trademark of Google LLC. Use of this trademark is subject to Google Permissions.

Children

The Extension is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
